Process factors that are influencing the post fortification methods were mentioned by four studies. Findings are distinguished in two subcategories:
\begin{itemize}
\item Which fortification substance was used and
\item Which fortification method was used 
\end{itemize}
The findings also showed that the used substance and method can interact between each other, therefore weaken or strengthen the initial effect. For instance, this is shown by \citet{noodle} with instant noodles that underwent fortification process through addition of catfish powder with the pasta being tray dried or deep-fat fried. Resulting in the highest values of cooking loss when deep-fat frying and addition of catfish powder was combined.
