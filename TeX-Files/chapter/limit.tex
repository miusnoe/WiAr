As with any research, this term paper has limitations. A major part is attributed to the limitations of the included studies themselves, which investigated different food matrices and also different methodological approaches. Also, studies often rely on accelerated shelf life tests and lack data on real long-term storage. Additionally, the scope was limited to recent findings since 2020 and excluded fruits. Thus, this term paper covers highly relevant but not exhaustive food matrices. Finally, it should be considered that multiple studies investigated different factors simultaneously. The effects of interactions between those factors make comparability difficult.
