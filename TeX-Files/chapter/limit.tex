As with any research, this term paper has certain limitations. A major part arises from the limitations of the included studies themselves, which examined different food matrices and employed varying methodological approaches. Many studies relied on accelerated shelf life tests and therefore lack data on real long-term storage. Furthermore, the scope of this paper was restricted to recent findings published since 2020 and excluded fruit matrices. Consequently, the work covers highly relevant but not exhaustive food categories. It should also be noted that several studies investigated multiple factors simultaneously, and the resulting interaction effects make direct comparability between studies challenging.
